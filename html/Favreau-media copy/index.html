Recent News

On October 28, after a rough 104-103 loss to the New York Knicks, Power Forward Patrick Williams sustained a serious wrist injury and will be out 4-6 months after his surgery. This opens up minutes for Javonte Green, Derrick Jones Jr. and Ayo Dosunmu.

Upcoming News

Point Guard Coby White is set to return on Nov. 15th against the Lakers. The 21 year old guard has missed the first 13 games of the season with a shoulder injury. He should see less minutes as last year as he is now playing behing the newly acquired Lonzo Ball who has had a hot start to the season. White will ease his way back into the lineup so in the first few games of his return he will not see much playing time.
